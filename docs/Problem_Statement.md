Problem

Manufacturing managers often spend significant time gathering information from disconnected production, quality, and inventory systems before making operational decisions. This fragmented workflow delays decision-making, reduces operational visibility, and makes it difficult to prioritize the actions that have the greatest business impact.

Although factories generate large volumes of operational data, managers still manually interpret reports from multiple systems to answer questions such as:

What is today's biggest operational risk?
Which production line requires attention?
Is inventory likely to impact production?
What should I prioritize today?

Current dashboards provide data but rarely deliver coordinated, actionable recommendations.

Proposed Solution

FactoryOps AI is a Multi-Agent Manufacturing COO built with Google Agent Development Kit (ADK).

The system coordinates specialized AI agents responsible for production, quality, and inventory analysis. These agents retrieve operational data through MCP, execute reusable manufacturing skills, and generate executive-level recommendations with human approval and evaluation.

Expected Benefits
Faster operational decision-making
Reduced manual analysis
Unified operational visibility
Prioritized executive recommendations
Demonstration of enterprise AI agent architecture
