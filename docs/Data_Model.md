# FactoryOps AI Data Model

## Input Datasets

| Dataset | Purpose | Used By |
|---------|---------|---------|
| Production | Manufacturing performance metrics | Operations Agent |
| Predictive Maintenance | Machine health and predictive scores | Operations Agent |
| Inventory | Material stock levels and lead times | Inventory Agent |
| Quality | Defects, repair costs, quality metrics | Quality Agent |

---

## Production Report

Generated by the Operations Agent.

Contains:

- Total Machines
- Average Speed
- Average Defect Rate
- Average Error Rate
- Average Predictive Score
- Efficiency Distribution
- Status
- Recommendation

---

## Inventory Report

Generated by the Inventory Agent.

Contains:

- Total Materials
- Low Stock Count
- Low Stock Materials
- Average Lead Time
- Status
- Recommendation

---

## Quality Report

Generated by the Quality Agent.

Contains:

- Total Defects
- Critical Defects
- Average Repair Cost
- Defect Types
- Status
- Recommendation

---

## Factory Health Report

Generated by the Factory Health Tool.

Contains:

- Overall Status
- Top Priority
- Executive Summary
- Production Report
- Inventory Report
- Quality Report
- Executive Decision

---

## Executive Decision

Generated by the Executive Decision Engine.

Contains:

- Risk Level
- Business Priority
- Recommended Action
- Approval Required
- Estimated Business Impact
- Reason
